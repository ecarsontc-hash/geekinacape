Pre-Session Questionnaire

Before we meet, a few questions help me arrive already thinking about your specific situation. In attempting to touch on everything, there are 9 sections to the form, but each is short. Complete it using whichever of the three methods below is best for you.

How to complete the form

The Pre-session questionnaire is available below.
If you would prefer to open it full screen (for example if working on a smaller screen) click "Full page version" above.
If you prefer to fill in by hand or using a word processor, choose "Fill in manually" above.
If filling in manually, please return it to reachingout@geekinacape.com — if printing out, you can scan or photograph it clearly before emailing.

There are no right or wrong answers. If you don't know something, 'not sure' is a perfectly good response, and actually quite helpful.
